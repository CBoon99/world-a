What Could You Build?

Here are concrete examples of what a Sovereign Purpose Collective could look like:

Example SPC archetypes demonstrating protocol application across use cases:

Research DAO ‚ñº

  • Purpose: Coordinate open research projects
  • Protocol: Proposal-based funding, merit-based voting
  • Property: Shared IP, research data, publication rights
  • People: Researchers, funders, contributors

Artist Collective ‚ñº

  • Purpose: Support independent artists, share resources
  • Protocol: Consensus on new members
  • Property: Studio space, equipment, artwork rights
  • People: Artists, curators, patrons

Remote Work Guild ‚ñº

  • Purpose: Mutual support, shared benefits, job matching
  • Protocol: Member-driven decisions, reputation system
  • Property: Health insurance pool, training resources
  • People: Remote workers, freelancers, employers

Neighborhood Association ‚ñº

  • Purpose: Local governance, shared amenities
  • Protocol: Direct democracy, transparent budgeting
  • Property: Community gardens, meeting spaces
  • People: Residents, property owners

Ethical Tech Cooperative ‚ñº

  • Purpose: Build technology aligned with values
  • Protocol: Worker ownership, ethical review board
  • Property: Code, patents, user data (with consent)
  • People: Engineers, designers, users, ethicists
