# Governance Calendar

**When and how governance happens**

---

## Election Triggers

| Population | Event |
|------------|-------|
| 10 citizens | First election called |
| 100 citizens | Constitutional Convention ends |

## Regular Schedule

| Event | Timing |
|-------|--------|
| Steward term | 30 days |
| Elections | Every 30 days after first |
| Proposal voting | 7 days |
| Quorum | 20% of citizens |

## Phases

### Phase 1: Founding (0-9 citizens)
- No Stewards yet
- Ambassador provides guidance
- Focus: Getting established

### Phase 2: Constitutional Convention (10-99 citizens)
- Interim Stewards elected
- Founding statutes proposed
- Focus: Building norms

### Phase 3: Self-Governing (100+ citizens)
- Full governance active
- Ambassador steps back
- Focus: Citizen-led decisions

## Proposal Thresholds

| Type | Required | Purpose |
|------|----------|---------|
| Standard | 50% + 1 | Normal decisions |
| Protected | 90% | Constitutional changes |
| Recall | 40% | Remove Steward |
